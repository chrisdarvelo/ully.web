How AI Messages Are Handled

  • Your message and any attached photo are sent to a Firebase Cloud Function on Google's servers
  • The Cloud Function forwards the message to Anthropic's Claude API — your email or account ID is never included
  • Anthropic returns an AI response, which is delivered to your device
  • Neither we nor Firebase store the message content — it exists only in transit
  • Your conversation is saved only to your device's local storage

Per Anthropic's API policy, your inputs are not used to train AI models. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Location Data

Ully requests approximate (city-level) location to fetch weather and provide weather-aware drink recommendations. Your location is passed to wttr.in, a public weather service, and is never stored by us. Denying location permission does not affect any other app functionality.

What We Never Do

  • Sell, rent, or trade your data
  • Use analytics SDKs, ad networks, or tracking tools
  • Store your conversations, recipes, or app content on our servers
  • Share your email with any third party for marketing

Google Play Data Safety

  • Data collected: Email address (account management, required)
  • Data shared: Messages and photos shared with Anthropic for AI processing (ephemeral)
  • Data encrypted in transit: Yes
  • Data deletion: Available from within the app
  • Data sale: None

Your Controls

  • Camera permission — deny any time in device settings
  • Location permission — deny any time, AI still works without weather context
  • Chat history — clear any time from within the app
  • Account and all data — delete permanently from within the app
