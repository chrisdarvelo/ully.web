Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email. A reset link will arrive within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't appear.

Why isn't the AI answering my question?

Ully AI is designed exclusively for coffee topics. Questions outside of coffee, brewing, equipment, or technique won't receive a response. Try rephrasing around a coffee context.

I hit the daily AI limit.

Ully enforces a daily message limit for fair access. Your limit resets at midnight. If you believe you hit the limit in error, email us at support@ullycoffee.com.

I lost my recipes. Can they be recovered?

Recipes are stored on your device only. If you deleted the app or reset your phone, they cannot be recovered — they were never on our servers. We recommend keeping backups of important recipes.

The Dial-in camera isn't working.

Go to Settings → Ully → Camera on your device and enable access. Restart the app after granting permission.

Does Ully store my conversations?

No. Chat history is stored only on your device. We have no access to your conversations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile tab, scroll to the bottom, and tap Delete Account. See the full Account Deletion guide.

How do I report a bad AI response?

Tap the report option below the message in chat. For serious issues, email us at support@ullycoffee.com with a description of the response.

Still need help?

Email support@ullycoffee.com with your account email, device model, OS version, and a description of the issue.
