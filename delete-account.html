Delete from the App

The fastest way. Takes less than a minute.

01

Open the Ully app and sign in.

02

Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar.

03

Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.

04

Enter your password to confirm.

05

Tap Delete. Your account and all data are permanently removed.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account and login credentials
  • Any files stored in your account (Firebase Storage)
  • All local data on your device — profile, recipes, cafes, follows, chat history

Because all app data is stored on your device, deletion is immediate and complete. There is no server-side database to purge separately.

Can't Access the App?

Email us with the account email address. We'll process your request within 30 days.

support@ullycoffee.com

After Deletion

  • Your account cannot be recovered
  • You may create a new account at any time with the same or a different email
